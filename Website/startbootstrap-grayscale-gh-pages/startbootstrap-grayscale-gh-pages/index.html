About Us

We are a HOI IV community serving PC gamers who love HOI . Our Community is a group of semi-hardcore and casual players who have gathered together under a single banner to game together and build lasting friendships! Each of us has a passion for gaming and we are always looking to expand our ranks across all our groups. Our community is home to a variety of games from MMORPGs to Tabletop RPGs! As you can see we support several games, each with a 50% or better unique playerbase. So while there are members part of many groups, you can count on there being a dedicated group for each game. We are always looking to host new games to build and maintain solid teams and friendships which will make their mark on the gaming world. To achieve this we are always on the hunt for talented players as well as talented leaders! If that's you, check us out on our Discord server and we can talk!

Active Community

One of the great things about our community is that we are active both on our forums and Discord and usually always have someone to play with. By being so active in many games, those that grow tired of one game always have friends to play with on another.

Awarding Members

What makes us different from other places is you will not have any teens telling you what to do. Instead we reward members with what they do within the community, and it is both shown on our website AND Discord itself (which no other community does both).

Fair & Honest Leadership

Known for well admined server, offering fair investigations and a non abusive admin team, players know when they play on a HOI IV server they are safe. We also go above and beyond in which we display all our donations out in the open from where they go and even the paypal in which they sit, no other community is this open.
